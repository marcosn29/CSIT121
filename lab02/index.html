follow my recipe and you will have the best burrito ever!

The best chicken burrito

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breast
  • 1 cup of tomato rice
  • 1/2 cup of black beans
  • lettuce
  • pico de gallo
  • ranch
  • large flour tortilla

recipe

  1. Grill the chicken with whatever seasoning you like
  2. Cook the rice, i prefer spanish rice which is just tomato rice but you can use whatever rice you may like
  3. Pico de gallo is a mixture of tomatos, onions, cilantro, and squeezed lime juice. I like to add this to my burritos because it adds a tangy flavor
  4. Add ranch
  5. Put everything onto the middle of flour tortilla and fold sides while flattening the filling. Then pinch the bottom in and roll tightly and you got yourself a chicken burrito!

When i started going to the gym i realized in order for me to have a good workout i needed something nutritious. I searched all around the web but i couldnt seem to find something that i could take with me on the go. I started cooking chicken with rice and beans before the gym but i was running late because my friend was picking me up to go to the gym so i threw everything onto a flour tortilla, added a couple of things i like and after that i fell in love with my chicken burrito it is easy and its full of protein.
